Our Story?

Meraj, an IIT post graduate, launched the startup 'Prodigy', an offline coaching center in the early 2019. It was Mumbai-based and taught Physics, Chemistry, Maths and Biology to the students of XI and XII for competitive exams. But soon, as the deadly pandemic shifted the world in online mode, Meraj knew that he had to relaunch Prodigy online with wider range of subjects to improve skills of so many people who had lost their jobs or are unable to get one in these tough times, with ambitions outside of secondary school level science. Meraj got in touch with his old friends and classmates who were now pursuing PhD degrees in many fields to launch a superior version of Prodigy to keep up with the worldly pace, and meet the various demands of the hiring companies
Today, more than ___ students are enrolled in more than ____ courses. Prodigy boasts mentors from top reputed institutions of the country to guide students on the right path of success. Be it learning for a passion, or purpose, Prodigy serves as the best online coaching in the country to meet all your learning needs. We aim to raise the skills of every student at the best level possible, regardless of their their undergraduate institutions to bridge the gap in this skill-based society. Students from prodigy have cracked top companies in the I.T. Sector such as Microsoft, Amazon in their both on campus and off campus placements.
"The students of the future will demand the learning support that is appropriate for their situation or context. Nothing more, nothing less."
